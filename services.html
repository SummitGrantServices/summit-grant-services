Services

Grant and funding support across the full lifecycle.

Summit Grant Services helps organizations move from funding uncertainty to a clear, actionable strategy — then supports the research, writing, submission, compliance, and management work needed to pursue and administer funding effectively.

Core services.

Our work is designed for organizations that need more than a writer. We operate as a strategic funding partner that can integrate with leadership, finance, program, and development teams as needed.

Grant Research

We identify federal, state, local, foundation, corporate, and philanthropic funding opportunities aligned with your organization’s mission, eligibility, geography, project type, and timing.

Grant Strategy

We help determine which opportunities are worth pursuing, how to position the project, what partnerships may strengthen the application, and how to build a practical funding pipeline.

Grant Writing

We prepare compelling, compliant proposal narratives, executive summaries, work plans, attachments, and supporting documents tailored to funder requirements.

Budget Development

We support budget narratives, cost allocation logic, match documentation, sustainability planning, and alignment between project scope and financial request.

Submission Management

We help manage deadlines, portals, required forms, attachments, registrations, and final review so that submissions are complete, timely, and responsive.

Grant Management

We support post-award reporting, reimbursement processes, compliance tracking, funder communication, documentation, performance monitoring, and closeout.

Advanced advisory services.

For complex initiatives, Summit provides specialized support that strengthens project design, funder confidence, and long-term implementation readiness.

Program Evaluation

Evaluation planning, performance measures, logic models, outcome tracking, and reporting frameworks that align with funder expectations and program goals.

Social Impact Analysis

Support defining, measuring, and communicating the social value of programs, investments, and community-based initiatives.

Due Diligence Evaluation

Assessment of project readiness, funder fit, implementation capacity, compliance risk, documentation gaps, and strategic viability before major funding pursuits.

Triple Bottom Line Strategy

Funding and project strategy that accounts for people, planet, and long-term financial sustainability.

Grant Systems Support

Assistance navigating grant portals, funder systems, reporting platforms, reimbursement workflows, registrations, and documentation procedures.

Fractional Grant Team Support

Flexible support for organizations that need experienced grant professionals without building a full internal grants department.

Built for both institutional and mission-driven clients.

Summit is structured to support large companies, public agencies, nonprofits, healthcare providers, community-based organizations, agricultural enterprises, and complex partnerships.

For Executives

Clear funding strategy, opportunity prioritization, and practical guidance for deciding where to invest time, staff capacity, and organizational resources.

For Project Managers

Hands-on support translating project ideas into fundable scopes, timelines, deliverables, budgets, and implementation plans.

For Finance & Compliance Teams

Careful attention to budget structure, allowability, documentation, reporting, reimbursement, and post-award compliance requirements.

How engagements typically work.

Every client is different, but most engagements follow a practical sequence designed to reduce confusion and focus effort on the right opportunities.

1. Discovery

We learn about your organization, priorities, current funding needs, capacity, and timing.

2. Opportunity Review

We assess available funding options and identify the strongest-fit opportunities.

3. Strategy & Work Plan

We define the recommended pursuit strategy, timeline, responsibilities, and materials needed.

4. Development & Submission

We prepare, review, refine, and submit complete grant packages or support your team through submission.

5. Post-Award Support

When funding is awarded, we can help manage reporting, compliance, documentation, and closeout.
