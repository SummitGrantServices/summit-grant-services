Sectors

Grant expertise for organizations with complex funding needs.

Summit Grant Services supports clients across sectors where public funding, private philanthropy, compliance, program design, financial management, and measurable impact all matter.

Sectors we serve.

Our team helps organizations identify the right opportunities, shape fundable projects, prepare competitive applications, and manage awarded funds with care.

Nonprofits & Community-Based Organizations

We support nonprofits working in human services, community development, aging services, youth programs, housing, food security, workforce, public safety, and other mission-driven areas.

Healthcare & Behavioral Health

We help healthcare providers, rural health organizations, behavioral health providers, clinics, and community health initiatives pursue funding for access, workforce, prevention, care coordination, and service expansion.

Agriculture & Food Systems

We support producers, agricultural businesses, food systems organizations, conservation initiatives, rural development projects, supply chain improvements, and innovation-focused agricultural work.

Government & Public Agencies

We assist local governments, regional agencies, public authorities, and public-sector partners with grant research, application development, compliance planning, and post-award management.

Education & Workforce

We help schools, colleges, workforce organizations, training providers, and education partners pursue funding for programming, facilities, career pathways, apprenticeships, and student support.

Community & Economic Development

We support projects involving infrastructure, housing, downtown revitalization, economic mobility, small business ecosystems, resilience, transportation, and place-based community investment.

Energy, Environment & Conservation

We assist with funding strategies for conservation, water, resilience, energy efficiency, clean technology, environmental restoration, and sustainability-focused projects.

Research, Science & Innovation

With access to Ph.D.-level expertise across scientific fields, Summit can support research-driven, technical, innovation, and commercialization-oriented funding pursuits.

Corporate & Institutional Initiatives

We help companies and institutional teams evaluate non-dilutive funding, public-private partnerships, government programs, foundation alignment, and impact-oriented funding strategies.

Built for organizations at different stages of funding readiness.

Some clients come to Summit with a specific grant in mind. Others need help understanding what is possible, what is realistic, and where to begin.

Emerging Organizations

We help newer organizations clarify funder fit, strengthen documentation, develop a grant strategy, and prepare for competitive opportunities.

Established Nonprofits & Agencies

We support organizations with ongoing grant needs, complex programs, multiple funding streams, reporting obligations, and capacity constraints.

Large Institutions & Companies

We help larger teams assess opportunity fit, coordinate technical content, manage timelines, and pursue competitive public or philanthropic funding aligned with strategic goals.

Cross-sector expertise matters.

Many strong funding opportunities sit at the intersection of sectors: healthcare and housing, agriculture and conservation, education and workforce, infrastructure and resilience, or nonprofit service delivery and government priorities.

Integrated Project Design

We help clients frame projects in ways that align with funder priorities, community needs, measurable outcomes, and implementation realities.

Partnership Strategy

We identify where partnerships, letters of support, referral systems, data sharing, subawards, or collaborative models may strengthen a funding request.

Compliance Awareness

We support grant development with attention to eligibility, allowable costs, reporting, match, procurement, documentation, and post-award requirements.
