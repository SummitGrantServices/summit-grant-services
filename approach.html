Our Approach

Strategic, practical, and integrated grant support.

Summit Grant Services helps clients move from opportunity identification to funded implementation with a clear process, disciplined project management, and practical support at every stage of the funding lifecycle.

We start with strategy, not paperwork.

A strong grant application begins before writing starts. Summit helps clients understand the opportunity, clarify the project, assess funder fit, and define a realistic path to submission and post-award success.

Focused on Fit

We evaluate whether an opportunity aligns with the client’s mission, eligibility, geography, project scope, timing, match requirements, capacity, and long-term goals.

Built Around the Team

We integrate with executive directors, project managers, finance teams, program staff, evaluators, technical experts, and partners to make the process easier and more organized.

Designed for Implementation

We develop proposals with attention to what happens after award: reporting, compliance, documentation, reimbursement, measurable outcomes, and funder communication.

The Summit process.

Our process is flexible enough to meet the needs of different organizations, but structured enough to keep complex funding pursuits moving forward.

Step 01

Discovery and Readiness Review

We begin by understanding the organization, project goals, current funding needs, available documentation, staff capacity, timelines, and decision-making process.

Step 02

Funding Landscape Analysis

We assess the relevant funding landscape, including government grants, state pass-through funds, local opportunities, foundations, corporate philanthropy, and strategic funding pathways.

Step 03

Opportunity Prioritization

We help determine which opportunities are strongest, which are premature, which are too risky, and which deserve focused pursuit based on timing, competitiveness, eligibility, and fit.

Step 04

Project Strategy and Positioning

We refine the project concept, define the need, shape the funding request, identify outcomes, align the budget, and clarify the case for support.

Step 05

Proposal Development

We prepare narratives, budgets, budget justifications, work plans, evaluation plans, letters of support, attachments, forms, and other required submission materials.

Step 06

Review, Compliance, and Submission

We support final review, portal navigation, completeness checks, compliance alignment, deadline management, and submission coordination.

Step 07

Post-Award Support

After award, we can help with reporting, reimbursement, documentation, performance tracking, compliance monitoring, funder communication, and closeout.

We make complex funding pursuits easier to manage.

Many grant opportunities require more than writing. They require coordination, technical understanding, compliance awareness, budget logic, attachments, approvals, partners, and a clear implementation plan.

Executive Clarity

Leadership receives a clear recommendation about whether to pursue, what the risks are, what will be required, and how the opportunity fits the broader funding strategy.

Project Management Discipline

We help organize deadlines, assignments, required inputs, drafts, reviews, attachments, letters, budgets, and submission steps.

Compliance Awareness

We review requirements carefully so applications are not only persuasive, but also responsive, complete, allowable, and aligned with funder expectations.

How we integrate with client teams.

Summit can serve as a behind-the-scenes extension of your team or as a more visible strategic advisor, depending on the engagement.

For organizations with limited grant capacity

We can provide structure, research, writing, budgeting support, and deadline management so executive directors, project leads, and finance staff are not forced to carry the entire process alone.

For organizations with internal grant teams

We can support overflow work, complex applications, specialized research, technical narratives, review, strategy, evaluation planning, or post-award management.

For companies and institutions

We can help translate technical, operational, scientific, or business concepts into funder-ready proposals with clear public value, impact, budget logic, and implementation plans.

For collaborative projects

We can help coordinate partners, define roles, develop letters of support, clarify subaward structures, align outcomes, and present the collaboration clearly to funders.
