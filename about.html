We help clients make sense of complex funding opportunities.

Grants can be powerful, but they are rarely simple. Funding opportunities often involve eligibility rules, strict deadlines, budget requirements, compliance obligations, technical narratives, scoring criteria, partnerships, documentation, and post-award management. Summit helps clients navigate that complexity with strategy, structure, and experienced support.

Strategic

We help clients decide which opportunities are worth pursuing and how to position projects for the strongest possible fit with funder priorities.

Practical

We organize the work, clarify the requirements, develop the materials, and make the process easier for busy leadership, program, and finance teams.

Integrated

We can work as an extension of the client’s team, supporting research, writing, budgeting, submission, compliance, reporting, and post-award management.

Who we support.

Summit works with organizations that need experienced funding support but may not have the time, staff capacity, or specialized expertise to manage every part of the grant process internally.

Nonprofits and Community Organizations

We help mission-driven organizations pursue funding for programs, services, facilities, capacity building, outreach, evaluation, and community impact.

Companies and Institutional Teams

We support businesses, research teams, healthcare providers, agricultural enterprises, and large institutions pursuing non-dilutive funding, public funding, foundation alignment, and strategic partnerships.

Public Agencies and Government Partners

We assist public-sector and quasi-public organizations with grant development, compliance planning, documentation, reporting, and implementation support.

Collaborative and Multi-Partner Projects

We help organize partnerships, define roles, build shared narratives, coordinate attachments, and present complex projects clearly to funders.

What makes Summit different.

Many organizations can draft a grant application. Summit is built to help clients think through the full funding strategy, from fit and readiness to submission and implementation.

We evaluate fit before pursuit.

We help clients avoid chasing weak-fit opportunities and focus effort on grants and funders that align with their mission, timing, capacity, and goals.

We understand implementation.

We draft with post-award realities in mind, including reporting, reimbursements, compliance, documentation, funder communication, and measurable outcomes.

We adapt to the client.

Some clients need a full grant team. Others need research, review, strategy, budgeting, evaluation planning, or overflow support. Summit can integrate where needed.
